Call for Workshop Proposals

12th International Conference on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (BDA 2024)
December 17-20, 2024, Hyderabad, India
https://scis.uohyd.ac.in/~bda2024/

The BDA 2024 Organizing Committee invites proposals for half-day and full-day workshops on established and emerging topics in the area of Big Data, AI and related areas. The goal of BDA 2024 workshops is to provide a forum for researchers and practitioners from academia and industry in the related domains to share novel ideas, research challenges and issues, and innovative next-generation applications in any topic related to Big Data and AI with the intent of stimulating interesting research in this area.

Workshop proposals can be in any established or emerging domain related to Big Data, AI and related topics (For topics, please refer the CFP of BDA 2024 conference https://scis.uohyd.ac.in/~bda2024/cfp.html) Workshop proposals are also welcome in the interdisciplinary areas such as Big Data or Data Science based problems and research challenges & opportunities in domains including (but not limited to) agriculture, education, open distance learning, transportation, smart cities, skills development, climate change, bioinformatics, environmental management, finance and healthcare.

The conference will provide all the necessary facilities for organizing the workshops such as presentation rooms. Workshop chairs will autonomously organize the workshops including the workshop program committee selection, review processes and paper acceptance/rejection and publication.


Instructions for submission:
Workshop proposals should be of maximum three pages and contain the following information: Title of the workshop, Objectives and Scope, Target audience and expected number of attendees, Names, email addresses and webpages of the prospective workshop organizers, A list of Program Committee members (tentative), A preliminary workshop format (either half-day or full-day) and tentative timeline.

Workshop proposals should be submitted via email to the following workshop chairs at Chinmaya Kumar Dehury (chinmaya.dehury@ut.ee) and Saurabh Shukla (saurabh.shukla@iiitl.ac.in).

Important Dates:
• Workshop Proposal Submission Deadline: May 31, 2024
• Workshop Notification: June 30, 2024

Workshop Chairs:
• Chinmaya Kumar Dehury, University of Tartu, Estonia
• Saurabh Shukla, IIIT Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India
• Prasan Kumar Sahoo, Chang Gung University, Taoyuan, Taiwan

For any queries and clarifications regarding the workshops, please send an email to Chinmaya Kumar Dehury (chinmaya.dehury@ut.ee) and Saurabh Shukla (saurabh.shukla@iiitl.ac.in).

Call for Tutorials

12th International Conference on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (BDA 2024)
University of Hyderabad, Telangana, India, 500046
December 17 - 20, 2024
https://scis.uohyd.ac.in/~bda2024

The BDA 2024 Organizing Committee invites proposals for half-day and full-day tutorials on established and emerging topics in the area of Big Data, Artificial Intelligence and related areas (Please refer to the conference web site for the topics). Each proposal should clearly specify the subject matter, a detailed outline of the major topics covered, goals of the tutorial and the intended audience.

Instructions for submission:

A submission should include the following details: Title of the tutorial, Presenters (Name, Affiliation and Brief Bio), an abstract with maximum limit of 400 words, Duration (half day/ full day), Target audience, and how the intended target audience would benefit from the tutorial. The submissions can be submitted in a Word or PDF format.

The submissions can be sent by e-mail to both tutorial chairs (jimson@iitp.ac.in and abhishek.hazra1@gmail.com).

Tutorial Chairs:
Jimson Mathew (IIT Patna),
Abhishek Hazra (IIIT Sri City)

Important Dates:
Proposal submission Due: Aug 4, 2024
Acceptance Notification: Aug 26, 2024
*All deadlines are 23:59 Indian Standard Time (IST)

For any queries and clarifications regarding the tutorial, please send an email to the tutorial chairs (jimson@iitp.ac.in and abhishek.hazra1@gmail.com).
